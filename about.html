About NextMove India 🌟

We started with one dream: every Indian child should have the confidence to speak, lead, and shine.

Our Mission

Why We Started NextMove India

India produces millions of brilliant minds — but many children grow up feeling scared to speak in English, afraid to raise their hand, or nervous about facing a crowd. We knew this had to change.

NextMove India was founded by passionate educators who believe that confidence is a skill, not a talent. With the right environment, every child can learn to express themselves boldly and beautifully.

Our curriculum blends modern public speaking techniques, child psychology principles, and fun interactive learning to create a truly transformational experience for every child we teach.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values 💎

🧒

Child-Centred Learning

Every lesson, activity, and teacher interaction is designed keeping the child's emotional and developmental needs at the centre.

🤝

Partnership with Parents

We believe parents are our co-teachers. Weekly reports, open communication, and parent involvement are not optional — they're essential.

🌈

Joyful Environment

Learning happens best when children feel safe, happy, and excited. Our classes are designed to feel like the best part of a child's day.

📈

Measurable Progress

We set clear goals for every child and measure progress honestly. Parents always know what their child has learned and what comes next.

🛡️

Safety & Trust

Our platform, teachers, and processes are built around one principle: your child's safety and your peace of mind.

🌍

Accessible to All

Great education shouldn't be limited by location or budget. We work hard to keep our programs accessible to families across India.
