What We Teach

A Sneak Peek into Our Curriculum 📋

Week 1–2

Building Comfort

Getting to know the teacher and classmates. Icebreakers, fun games, and creating a safe space to speak without fear.

Week 3–4

First Words & Sentences

Guided conversations, picture descriptions, and simple storytelling to build initial speaking habits.

Week 5–8

Fluency Building

Daily practice activities, role plays, debates among peers, and vocabulary enrichment exercises.

Week 9–12

Public Speaking

Mini presentations, speeches, poetry recitation, and class "shows" where children perform for each other.
