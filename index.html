Child Hesitates to Speak English in School

They know the words inside but freeze when it is time to speak in class, with teachers, or in front of friends. This is very common and very fixable.

Shy Child Who Lacks Confidence to Speak

Avoids eye contact, does not raise their hand in class, and withdraws when adults ask them a question in English. Shyness is a habit — and we break it gently.

Teachers notice they do not participate in English activities — and it starts to affect grades, friendships, and overall school performance.
